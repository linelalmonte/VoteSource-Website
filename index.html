Types of Elections

Understand the elections that impact your community and the nation. Every vote matters!

President

Elect the leader of the nation every four years

Governors

Choose the head of your state government

State Judges

Decide on those who interpret state laws

U.S Senate

Vote for representatives who create federal laws

Secretary of State

Select officials overseeing elections and state records

Mayors

Elect leaders responsible for city administration.
